About

The Web and Mobile club is a collaborative club dedicated to sharing knowledge and building progressive, accessible products. We combine forward-thinking design strategies with new development technologies to create solutions for underrepresented communities.

Our club's objective is to provide Santa Cruz students access to cooperatively share industry-standard tech practices, and an environment to work on software development projects.
