Process

The Web and Mobile club is a collaboratively focused club dedicated to sharing knowledge and building progressive, accessible products. We combine forward-thinking and user-centric design strategies with new development technologies to create solutions for underrepresented communities.

Our club's objective is to provide Santa Cruz students access to cooperatively share industry-standard tech practices, and an environment to work on software development projects. We nourish a multidisciplinary skillset with our design and development teams.
